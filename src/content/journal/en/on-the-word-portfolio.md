---
title: On the Word "Portfolio"
date: 2026-02-27
excerpt: I've always resisted that word. Here's why, and what I call these instead.
---

Every time I introduce this site to someone, I get stuck on the word "portfolio." It's not that I dislike showing the work — it's the feeling that word carries, like a pile of things filed away into a folder, waiting to be reviewed and scored.

But these photos aren't a finished body of work to me. They're records, still being sorted, of whatever I was thinking at the time. Some are technically good. Some are technical failures I still kept, because the thing that went wrong happened to be right.

So on this site, I don't call it a "portfolio." Just "Work." One word shorter, but what's missing is that filed-away feeling. These are separate, individual things that just happen to live on the same page.
